Australian Games Industry

An overview of a growing industry

Why Games?

Games are a growing industry in Australia, and since the COVID-19 pandemic, people have been growing more and more accustomed with using the medium to connect with others not just within the country, but internationally as well. There have been many games created within Australia that have reached a worldwide audience, and with the help of the Interactive Games & Entertainment Association (IGEA) and its resources thry have collected, it has never been easier to be both a gamer and a game maker in Australia.
